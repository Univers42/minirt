# Materials

## Emissive
Self-luminous, the emissive is described by the `radiance scale` and its `color`
it emits a direct/inderect shade when the ray hits the front side (checks surface orientation)

> it is used to make light sources or glowing objects

## Phong
Diffuse and glossy specular lobes

## Mirror

## Reflective
Phong-based material with glossy (fuzzy) reflection rather than perfect mirror
