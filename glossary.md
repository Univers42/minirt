- FOV – Field of View
- AA – Anti-Aliasing
- BRDF – Bidirectional Reflectance Distribution Function
- BVH – Bounding Volume Hierarchy

- IO – Input/Output

- BTDF – Bidirectional Scattering Surface reflectance Distribution
